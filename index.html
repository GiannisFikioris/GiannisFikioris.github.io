About me

I am a second year PhD student in the theory group at Cornell University, working with Eva Tardos. I received my BSE from the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, National Technical University of Athens. I completed my undergraduate thesis with Dimitris Fotakis, titled Perturbation Stability for Mechanism Design.

From July 2019 to August 2020, I was working in NCSR “Demokritos”, and in particular in the Complex Event Recognition Group, advised by Alexander Artikis.

This allows a complex event recognition engine (RTEC) to identify various events more quickly and accurate.
